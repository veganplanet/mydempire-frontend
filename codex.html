The Codex of MydEmpire
Every empire begins with a single decision. In MydEmpire, you do not simply collect assets — you build territory, unlock industry, activate production, and shape a living economy on the Hive blockchain.

This Codex is your field guide. It will help you understand the world, the assets, and the path from your first pack to a functioning industrial empire.
🎁
1. Every Empire Starts with a Pack
Your journey begins with a pack. Each pack opens into the core building blocks of your future empire — Land, Blueprint, and EMP. Some packs may even reveal rarer strategic assets. Opening a pack is not just a reveal moment — it is the first step into empire creation.
🌍
2. Land Is Your Foundation
Land is where your industries rise. Different land tiers offer different levels of building capacity, which means your territory directly shapes your long-term growth. A stronger empire starts with stronger foundations.
📜
3. Blueprints Unlock Industry
Blueprints are the plans behind your expansion. They determine what kind of factory can be built on your land. Every blueprint is a strategic opportunity: what you build today affects your economic strength tomorrow.
🏭
4. Factories Bring Your Empire to Life
Once a blueprint is used, your land becomes productive. Factories are the living engine of MydEmpire — they generate EP, strengthen your position, and push your empire forward. Idle assets become active power when industry begins.
⚡
5. EMP Powers Progress
EMP is the energy of your economic machine. It supports upgrades, maintenance, and future strategic choices. Without EMP, an empire may exist — but it cannot truly evolve.
💠
6. EP Measures Your Industrial Weight
EP, or Empire Power, is the strength of your productive network. It reflects how powerful your economy has become and connects your empire to the larger reward logic of the game. A stronger industrial system means a stronger presence in the world of MydEmpire.
🛒
7. The Marketplace Creates Strategy
The marketplace is where players reshape their empires through trade. Assets can change hands, strategies can shift, and opportunities can emerge. It is not just a store of listings — it is part of the living economy.
👑
8. This Is Not Just Collection — It Is Expansion
MydEmpire is designed around growth, planning, and participation. The most successful players will not simply own the most assets — they will understand how to turn those assets into a working empire. Every land, every blueprint, every factory is a decision.
🚀
Your First Objective
Open your first pack. Study your land. Understand your blueprint. Build your first factory.

Once production begins, you are no longer just a player looking at assets — you are an empire builder.
