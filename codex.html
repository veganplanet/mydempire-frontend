Core Gameplay Loop

Pack → Land + Blueprint + EMP → Build Factory → Generate EP → Maintain Production → Upgrade Strategically → Earn Reward Share

MydEmpire is not about holding assets passively. Strength comes from converting land slots and blueprints into active factories that keep producing EP over time.

Factory EP is calculated from base production, land multiplier, blueprint level multiplier, and active relic boost. Higher land tiers increase both factory slots and production strength.

B5 Limitation

Each land can contain maximum 1 B5 factory.

Land defines how many factories you can operate. Higher-tier land increases your expansion capacity but does not guarantee higher efficiency without proper blueprint and upgrade strategy.

Blueprints

Blueprints define both the industry type and the starting strength of the factory you build. When used for construction, the blueprint is consumed.

Blueprint Levels

  • B1 → Basic
  • B2 → Improved
  • B3 → Advanced
  • B4 → High
  • B5 → Elite

Extra Blueprint Minting

Extra blueprint minting is allowed when your total available land slots exceed your current blueprint count.

  • Cost = 50% of current pack price (USD)
  • Converted to HIVE at time of mint
  • Equivalent EMP is deducted

All EMP spent on blueprint minting supports the treasury and strengthens long-term reward sustainability.

Factories

A factory is created by combining one land slot, one blueprint, and EMP. After construction, it becomes a persistent production asset inside your empire.

How Factory EP is Calculated

Base EP × Land Multiplier × Blueprint Multiplier × Factory Level × Relic Boost

Factory production is determined by multiple strategic layers working together. Higher land tiers provide stronger production multipliers, stronger blueprints increase industrial efficiency, factory upgrades improve long-term output, and relics can provide additional boost bonuses.

System Effect on Production
Land Tier Controls base production multiplier and factory slots
Blueprint Level Higher blueprint tiers generate stronger production
Factory Level Upgrades increase total EP generation over time
Relics Provide additional percentage production boost

EMP utility

EMP is the core internal utility resource used for:

  • Factory setup
  • Upgrades
  • Maintenance
  • Extra blueprint minting

EMP from packs is soulbound gameplay utility. EMP purchased through the shop follows the fixed exchange reference of 100 EMP = 1 HIVE.

EP System

EP (Empire Production) measures the productive strength of your empire.

  • Generated by active factories
  • Boosted by relics
  • Used to determine reward share

EP is not stored or accumulated. It represents your real-time production power and directly affects your share of rewards.

Maintenance

Cycle

Each factory runs on a 7-day maintenance cycle.

How Payment Works

Maintenance is paid factory-by-factory in EMP for a fixed 7-day renewal. It is not a percentage claim on HIVE rewards.

Renewal Window

Renewal is allowed only in the final 2 days of the current cycle, creating a practical maximum of 9 days of remaining maintenance.

  • Factories stop producing when maintenance expires
  • No penalty beyond inactivity
  • Players must visit and renew factories individually
  • Maintenance exists to support active empire management, not passive automation

Empire Operations

Empire Operations are active industrial missions where players use EMP to run contracts, expand influence, and earn Industrial Authority.

Spend EMP → Run Operation → Wait Timer → Collect Result → Gain IA

Operation Types

Operation Required EP/day Duration Risk
Local Supply Contract 100 EP/day 3 hours Low
Regional Trade Route 250 EP/day 6 hours Moderate
Imperial Expansion Project 500 EP/day 12 hours High

Operations are designed as an EMP utility and engagement layer. Results may return EMP revenue, create a loss, or rarely award special assets.

Industrial Authority

Completed operations generate Industrial Authority (IA). IA represents your empire’s long-term operational reputation and unlocks milestone rewards as your empire becomes more active.

  • Local Supply gives smaller IA progress.
  • Regional Trade gives stronger IA progress.
  • Imperial Expansion gives the highest IA progress.

Rare Operation Rewards

  • Local Supply may rarely discover a B1 Blueprint.
  • Regional Trade may rarely award L1 Frontier land.
  • Imperial Expansion may rarely award L2 Estate land.

Rewards

MydEmpire does not print reward tokens. All rewards come from a real HIVE treasury funded by player activity and in-game economy.

Player Reward = (Player EP / Total EP) × Daily Reward Pool

Your Empire production (EP) determines your share — not luck, not farming.

  • Rewards are distributed based on EP share
  • Daily reward pool is calculated from treasury health
  • Rewards depend on your strategy, activity, and overall treasury health

HIVE Reward Accumulation

  • Unclaimed HIVE rewards accumulate for up to 7 days
  • On the 8th day, expired rewards return to the treasury
  • This keeps the system active and protects long-term sustainability

You are not earning tokens. You are earning a share.

Imperial Fragments

Imperial Fragments are collectible strategic materials obtained through crate rewards. Fragments may later be combined into premium empire assets and future expansion systems.

Daily crate activity rewards active empires and creates continuous gameplay progression beyond factory production alone.

Relics

  • +3% EP per relic
  • Maximum 2 counted
  • Total relic boost cap = 6%

Relics are rare strategic boosters and can also unlock premium convenience such as instant upgrade after active-day requirements are met.
