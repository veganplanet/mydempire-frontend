MydEmpire Economy
The MydEmpire economy is designed as a closed strategic loop. Packs create assets, assets build production, production creates EP, and EP connects players to a treasury-backed reward system. It is built around progression, planning and empire expansion — not endless inflation.
📦
Genesis Packs
Genesis Packs are the first gateway into the economy. They open into strategic assets including Land, Blueprint and EMP, with rare chances for special items.
🌍
Land & Slots
Land defines your empire’s industrial capacity. Higher-tier land gives more slots, which means more room for future factories and stronger long-term growth.
📜
Blueprints
Blueprints are construction assets. They decide what type of industry can be built and are consumed during development, making them strategically valuable.
🏭
Factories & EP
Factories are your production core. Active factories generate EP, and EP represents your economic weight in the empire.
⚡
EMP Utility
EMP powers important in-game decisions such as maintenance, growth and upgrades. It is a working utility layer, not just a passive balance number.
💎
Treasury & Rewards
Rewards are supported by treasury logic rather than uncontrolled inflation. This keeps the economy more disciplined and ties value to ecosystem health.
🛒
Marketplace Activity
The marketplace enables trade between players and adds an extra strategic layer beyond simple pack opening and asset holding.
👑
Long-Term Design
MydEmpire is not only about collecting NFTs. It is about building an industrial network, expanding influence, and developing a stronger empire over time.
