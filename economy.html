Genesis Packs
Genesis packs are the entry point into the economy. Each pack opens into core game assets such as Land, Blueprint and EMP, with a rare chance for special items like Relics. Pack sales help feed the broader treasury system.
Land & Slots
Land defines your empire’s industrial capacity. Higher tier lands provide more build slots, allowing you to place more factories and strengthen your production network over time.
Blueprints
Blueprints determine what kind of industry can be built. They are consumed when construction begins, making them a strategic asset rather than a decorative item.
Factories & EP
Factories are the main production units of the economy. Active factories generate EP, and EP represents your productive weight inside the system. A stronger industrial base means a stronger claim on future rewards.
EMP Utility
EMP is the internal power resource of MydEmpire. It is used for important in-game functions such as upgrades, maintenance and strategic progression. It is not just a number — it is a working utility layer of the empire.
Treasury & Rewards
Rewards are supported by treasury logic rather than endless inflation. This keeps the economy more controlled and ties player incentives to the long-term health of the ecosystem.
Marketplace Activity
The marketplace allows players to trade strategic assets and creates circulation between players. This supports economic activity beyond simple pack opening and adds another layer of decision-making.
Long-Term Design
The economy is designed to feel like an evolving empire system. Players do not simply collect items — they build production, manage progression, and participate in a wider strategic environment.
