On-Chain vs Off-Chain

A clear overview of which actions are recorded on the Hive blockchain and which live inside the MydEmpire game server.

✅ On-Chain = Needs Hive Keychain ⚙️ Off-Chain = Game Logic Only

Why this page exists

MydEmpire is a Web3 strategy game built on the Hive blockchain. Some actions directly touch your Hive wallet and are visible on-chain. Other actions are pure gameplay inside our backend (faster, no fees, no repeated wallet popups).

To stay transparent, we clearly list which actions are on-chain (you will see a Hive Keychain confirmation) and which are off-chain (stored in the MydEmpire game database).

Simple rule: if an action moves real HIVE between wallets, you will always see a Hive Keychain confirmation and it will be visible on-chain. Pure game mechanics (lands, factories, upgrades, EP, EMP balances) are handled off-chain.

Action overview

High-level overview of how different actions are handled.

Action Category Where it happens Keychain Popup Notes

Selling EMP for HIVE EMP economy On-chain HIVE payout to your wallet On-chain When you exit via EMP, the final HIVE payout to your wallet is on-chain.

Revenue distribution & treasury movements Platform On-chain transfers between MydEmpire accounts On-chain Pack sale revenue splits and treasury → reward wallet movements are executed as on-chain transfers by the admin.
Connecting wallet / login Access Off-chain (reading your Hive username / posting key) Off-chain No HIVE is moved. This step just lets the game know which Hive account you are playing with.
Opening packs (receiving LAND / BLUEPRINT / RELIC / EMP) Gameplay Off-chain (MydEmpire game database) Off-chain Random outcomes and NFT assignments are processed inside the game server to keep it fast and fee-less.
Building factories on lands Gameplay Off-chain Off-chain Consumes BLUEPRINT and EMP inside the game database. No HIVE leaves or enters your wallet.
Upgrading factories Gameplay Off-chain Off-chain Upgrade rules are enforced by the backend. No on-chain transaction is required.
Paying maintenance with EMP Gameplay Off-chain Off-chain EMP is deducted inside the game only. This does not touch your on-chain HIVE balance.
Claiming factory production (EMP) Gameplay Off-chain Off-chain EMP from your factories is credited to your in-game account. Converting EMP back to HIVE is a separate on-chain step.
EP (Empire Power) calculation Scoring Off-chain Off-chain All EP math is calculated on the server and used for rewards and leaderboards.
Leaderboards & seasonal rankings Scoring Off-chain Off-chain Leaderboards are built from the game database and refreshed periodically.
Notifications, logs & empire history UX & analytics Off-chain Off-chain Messages like “factory ready to upgrade” or “maintenance due” are tracked inside the game server only.

Why not put everything on-chain?

  • Too many popups: building, upgrading and claiming frequently would spam Hive Keychain.
  • Performance: pure game actions can be processed instantly.
  • Clean separation: on-chain is reserved for money & ownership; off-chain handles the simulation engine.

Our philosophy is simple: whenever real HIVE moves between wallets, it’s on-chain. Everything else is optimized to feel like a smooth strategy game.

Future on-chain features

As MydEmpire grows, some additional features may also be moved on-chain, for example:

  • Governance and voting (WRIT / RELIC holders influencing future rules)
  • Prestige NFTs or achievements
  • Snapshot-based events or seasonal reward claims

Whenever we add new on-chain actions, they will be documented here so you always know what is happening on Hive.
