🐀 The Rat Situation

🏭 Every thriving empire has a dirty secret...

As your factories operate, old machine parts, damaged equipment, forgotten tools, and industrial junk gradually pile up inside warehouse storage areas. If left unattended, the mess attracts rats 🐀. Cleanup crews can restore order and occasionally recover valuable Salvage Parts hidden among the clutter. 🔧
Cleanup Strategy
Warehouse Condition Rat Activity Cleanup Cost Salvage
🟢 Spotless 0 rats None None
🔵 Clean 1–3 rats Low Small chance
🟡 Orderly 4–8 rats Moderate Low
🟠 Cluttered 8–15 rats High Better
🔴 Infested 15–30 rats Highest Best
💡 Larger empires create more warehouse pressure over time. Regular cleanup keeps rat activity under control and may recover Salvaged Machinery Parts.
