🏭 Factory Fulfillment Guide

Factory Fulfillment Board lets your active industries complete real-feeling production orders over time.

1. Choose Fulfillment Type Standard Batch is easiest, Bulk Shipment is medium, and Grand Consignment is largest.
2. Choose Industry Your selected industry EP/day decides how fast progress grows.
3. Complete Target Progress = selected industry EP/day × elapsed days.
4. Claim Reward When target is complete, claim variable EMP plus fixed Fulfillment Credits. EMP outcome has 20% settlement tax deducted at claim. Fulfillment Credits are fixed and not affected by tax.
💡 Weak industries should usually choose Standard Batch. Strong industries can handle Bulk or Grand faster.
