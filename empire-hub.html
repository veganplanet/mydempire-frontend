🐀 The Rat Situation

🏭 Every thriving empire has a dirty secret...

As your factories operate, old machine parts, damaged equipment, forgotten tools, and industrial junk gradually pile up inside warehouse storage areas. If left unattended, the mess attracts rats 🐀. Cleanup crews can restore order and occasionally recover valuable Salvage Parts hidden among the clutter. 🔧
Cleanup Strategy

31+ Days 🔴 Infested Highest Best
