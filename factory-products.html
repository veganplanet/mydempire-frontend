What is an Imperial Ticket?

The Imperial Ticket is a permanent premium crafting asset created by burning selected Factory Goods and EMP.

Future uses may include:
  • Unlocking premium crafting recipes
  • Creating rare or guaranteed game assets
  • Accessing selected Imperial events
  • Future Marketplace trading

The Ticket does not expire. Its utility may expand as MydEmpire grows.
